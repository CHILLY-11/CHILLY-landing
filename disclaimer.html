Risk Disclaimer

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Cryptocurrency Risks

Cryptocurrencies, including World Mobile Token (WMTX), are high-risk investments. Their value can be extremely volatile and may drop significantly at any time. You should never invest money that you cannot afford to lose.

Staking Risks

Staking cryptocurrency carries specific risks, including but not limited to:

  • Potential loss of staked funds due to technical failures
  • Loss of liquidity while tokens are staked
  • Potential penalties or slashing of staked amounts
  • Fluctuations in reward rates over time

Reward Calculations

The staking calculator on this website provides estimations only. Actual rewards may vary significantly based on:

  • Network participation rates
  • Protocol changes
  • WMTX price fluctuations
  • Validator performance

Technical Risks

Earthnodes, blockchain technology, and decentralized systems carry inherent technical risks:

  • Smart contract vulnerabilities
  • Network attacks
  • Software bugs or errors
  • Protocol changes or updates

Regulatory Uncertainty

Cryptocurrency regulations are evolving globally. Future regulatory changes could impact the operation of Earthnodes and staking services, potentially affecting rewards or even the legality of staking in your jurisdiction.

Not Financial Advice

Information on this website is for educational purposes only. Nothing on this website constitutes financial, legal, or investment advice. Always consult with qualified professionals before making any investment decisions.

Do Your Own Research

Before staking WMTX or any cryptocurrency, thoroughly research the technology, risks, and potential rewards. Only you can determine if cryptocurrency staking is appropriate for your financial situation and risk tolerance.
